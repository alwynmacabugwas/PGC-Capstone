Inventory Asset Management System

Our portal offers a centralized database of establishments and inventory items, ensuring transparency and accessibility. Users can easily access information
on the essential supplies and equipment across provincial departments.
To ensure accuracy, please use correct spelling when searching for establishment names, product names, and other details. This helps maintain the integrity of the
database and ensures reliable information.
Together, let's promote transparency and accountability in inventory management within PGC to ensure the availability of safe and quality commodities for Cavite residents.
